About Me

I am Md. Ammar Hossain, an undergraduate student of Computer Science and Engineering at United International University (UIU). I am a passionate learner who enjoys exploring technical development, teaching, leadership, and community engagement.

As an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant, I have strengthened my communication, mentoring, and coordination skills by assisting students in lab sessions. I also served as an Editor at Aspect Series, where I enhanced my abilities in content management and professional communication.

My leadership experience includes working as the Head of Human Resources at UIU APP Forum and serving as a Campus Ambassador Campus Ambassador for ICT Olympiad Bangladesh (Season 2). I also contributed as an Organizer for UIU CSE Fest 2025, working with the Event and Logistics Team to ensure smooth event execution.

Technically, I have experience in C, C++, Java, Python, SQL, and web development using HTML, CSS, React, Node.js, PHP, Django, JavaFX, and Scene Builder. I am proficient with backend tools like MySQL and SQLite, and have hands-on hardware experience with Logisim, NI Multisim, and Arduino IDE. My workflow is supported by tools such as Git, Linux, VS Code, Google Colab, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Figma, Canva, and Jira. I also maintain an English typing speed of 30 WPM.

Across all my roles developer, mentor, organizer, ambassador, and editor, I strive to maintain professionalism, strong teamwork, and continuous learning. I aim to contribute meaningfully to future projects by combining my organizational, analytical, and technical strengths while gaining industry experience.
